Food Establishment Recommendations

Don Ishiyaki and Ramen

Located just a stone's throw away from the main campus building, Don Ishiyaki and Ramen is a cozy spot known for its mouthwatering ramen bowls and Japanese menu. Whether you're craving savory noodles or grilled meats, this restaurant has something for everyone.

Astro Dogs

Craving gourmet Korean corn dogs with a twist? Astro Dogs is the place to be. Located just steps away from campus, this unique eatery offers a variety of creative hot dog creations, loaded with innovative toppings and served in freshly baked buns.

Slices by Tony

Indulge in the variety of flavors of pizza at Slices by Tony. Conveniently located near campus, this restaurant offers a diverse menu.
